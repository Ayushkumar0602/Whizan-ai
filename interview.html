Help Center

Getting Started

Learn how to set up and conduct your first mock interview with AI Interview Pro.

1. Complete the setup form with your job role, target company, and resume.

2. Select your preferred interview type and difficulty level.

3. Choose an interviewer voice and avatar that you're comfortable with.

4. Click "Start Mock Interview" and begin your practice session.

Using the Interview Interface

The interview interface simulates a real interview experience with several helpful features:

- Click the microphone button to answer questions (your browser may ask for permission)

- Use the hint button if you're stuck on a question

- Skip questions if needed (but try to answer as many as possible)

- For coding interviews, use the built-in code editor with syntax highlighting

- For system design interviews, use the whiteboard to draw your architecture

Understanding Your Feedback

After each interview, you'll receive detailed feedback including:

- Overall score and category scores (technical, communication, etc.)

- Specific strengths and areas for improvement

- Personalized study recommendations

- Comparison to previous attempts (if applicable)
