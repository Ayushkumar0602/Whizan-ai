Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the handwriting conversion?
Our AI produces remarkably accurate results that closely resemble real handwriting. The custom handwriting option is particularly realistic as it's based on your actual writing samples.
Can I use this for math equations and diagrams?
Yes! Our converter handles mathematical notation, chemical formulas, and simple diagrams. For complex illustrations, we recommend adding them after conversion.
Is there a limit to how much text I can convert?
Our free version allows conversion of up to 10 pages at a time. For longer documents, the conversion will be split into multiple files.
How many handwriting samples do I need for custom font?
For best results, provide at least 2-3 paragraphs of your handwriting covering all letters, numbers, and common symbols. The more samples you provide, the more accurate the font will be.
Can I edit the converted handwritten document?
The output is an image/PDF, but you can make changes to your original document and convert it again. We're working on an editable handwritten format for future updates.
